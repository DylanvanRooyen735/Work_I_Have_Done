What do we do?

WOODROCK ANIMAL RESCUE PROUDLY RESCUE, REHABILITATE AND REHOMES.

We are the LEADING non-profit, pro-life, rescue, rehabilitation and re-homing shelter in South Africa. Woodrock Animal Rescue provides assistance to unfortunate, homeless, abused, neglected, stray, injured, ill, geriatric and unwanted animals (predominantly canines) that would otherwise have miserable lives and premature deaths; animals who have no voice of their own!
